non X settlement project

Kang Jin Hee , 2019


We‘ve been experiencing the rapid change of  our urban settlements replacing small houses with huge area occupied apartment development.




I planed to make an architectural urban planning about the current urban situation through the 3 phases I divided the whole complex issue into.
  1. Questioning
  2. Addressing
  3. The concensus

If we reject the view of a future modeled after valid value known from the past and drive this phenomenon to the extreme, it will eventually happen to produce outcomes that are not pre-determined.

I believe we could find some values of urban residence and establish the new settlement system with the social consensus emerging at that moment.
